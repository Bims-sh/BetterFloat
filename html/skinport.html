PRICES

Buff Price Reference

Sets the reference price for difference calculations on item prices.
Bid refers to the highest buy order price.
Ask refers to the lowest listing price. (Default)

Currency Conversion

Sets the reference rates for the currency conversion (USD -> X). Skinport will nevertheless always charge you in your local currency..
Real refers to the buff-like currency conversion rates. (Default)
Skinport refers to the rates used by Skinport, which can vary from Buff rates.

Show Steam Price

Show the Steam price of an item in addition to the Buff price.

Show Buff Price Difference

This settings allows to switch between displaying the difference to the Buff price in absolute units and the original difference to the Steam price in percentage.
